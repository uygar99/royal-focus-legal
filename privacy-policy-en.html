1. Introduction

Welcome to MyApp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

The personal information we collect may include:

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the application, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with you for customer service and updates

We may share your information in the following situations:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
